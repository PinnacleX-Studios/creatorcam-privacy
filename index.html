CreatorCam Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

CreatorCam ("the app") is a camera app developed by Sakshat Dewan ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app and the third-party services it uses collect, and how it's used.

The short version

CreatorCam has no backend server. Every video and audio recording you make is processed and stored entirely on your device — we never see it, upload it, or have access to it. The only data that leaves your device is handled by Google's advertising (AdMob) and billing (Google Play Billing) services, described below.

Camera, microphone, and your recordings

CreatorCam uses your device's camera and microphone, with your permission, to record video and audio. These recordings are saved only to your device's local storage and your device's photo/video gallery when you choose to save or share them. We do not collect, upload, or have any access to your photos, videos, or audio recordings.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

CreatorCam shows ads using Google AdMob to support free use of the app. AdMob may collect and share the following with Google for ad serving, measurement, and fraud prevention:

DataPurpose
Advertising ID / device identifiersAdvertising, ad measurement
App interaction data (ad views, clicks)Advertising, ad measurement

You can opt out of personalized advertising, or reset your advertising ID, in your device's system settings. See Google's own privacy policy for how AdMob handles this data: policies.google.com/privacy and Google's AdMob data disclosure.

In-app purchase (Google Play Billing)

CreatorCam offers an optional, one-time "Remove ads" purchase, processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see your payment details (card number, billing address, etc.) — that's handled by Google. The app only receives a purchase confirmation, which it uses locally on your device to unlock the ad-free, watermark-free experience. We do not store or share this purchase record with anyone.

No accounts, no personal information

CreatorCam does not require or support creating an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.

Data retention and deletion

Because we don't operate a backend or store any of your data ourselves, there's nothing on our end to delete. Your recordings stay on your device under your control, and you can manage advertising-related data (such as resetting or deleting your advertising ID) through your device's system settings.

Children's privacy

CreatorCam is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
